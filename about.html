About Energy World

Your trusted partner for solar energy solutions

Our Story

Energy World was founded with a simple vision: to make clean, renewable energy accessible to every home and business in Gujarat. What started as a small electrical shop has grown into Una's trusted name for solar solutions.

We specialize in providing high-quality solar products and professional installation services. From residential rooftop systems to agricultural solar pumps, we offer complete renewable energy solutions tailored to your needs.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to destination for solar energy in Una and surrounding areas.

Why Solar Energy?

Solar energy is clean, abundant, and cost-effective. By choosing solar, you're not only reducing your electricity bills but also contributing to a cleaner, greener future for generations to come.

Our Mission

To empower homes and businesses in Gujarat with reliable, energy-efficient solar solutions while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service. We strive to make modern solar technology accessible and affordable for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted solar energy provider in Gujarat, known for our extensive product range, expert guidance, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We aim to light up every home and power every dream with clean energy.
